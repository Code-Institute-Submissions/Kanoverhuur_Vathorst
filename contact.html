Parking

Due to the fact that we’re located in an urban area, there is limited public parking space available. We cannot guarantee parking immediately close to our premises, but chances are high you’ll find a space within 100m of our location. We encourage you to come by bike or public transport.

Train:

From Amersfoort Central Station, take the sprinter to Amersfoort Vathorst. From the station, it’s 700 meters to our location. You can plan your train trip at the NS website.

Bus:

From Amersfoort Central Station: take bus 3, 5 or 7 and leave the bus at the Damespolder stop. From there, it’s 200 meters to our location. Plan your trip at OV9292.
