About Us

Kanoverhuur Vathorst offers an unprecedented canoe and kayak adventure. With easy access to the stunning flora and fauna of the Arkemheen Polder and situated at the northern border of the medieval city of Amersfoort, Vathorst provides for an exciting natural and architectural experience. It is exactly this combination of a rich history, gorgeous nature and modern architecture that makes this district so popular.

Book one of our stable and comfortable kayaks or canoes and we'll guarantee you a truly unique day out!
